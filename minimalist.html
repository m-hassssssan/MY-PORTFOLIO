My Story

From Curiosity to Creation

My journey into the world of development started with a simple question: "How does this work?" That curiosity led me down a path of endless learning, from my first "Hello World" to building complex, interactive web applications.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with amazing teams and clients, each project teaching me something new. I believe that great design is invisible—it just works, feels natural, and brings joy to users.

Beyond the screen, I'm an avid photographer who loves capturing street scenes and landscapes. I find that creativity flows between code and camera—both require seeing the world differently and finding beauty in the details.
